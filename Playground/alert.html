Quick Reference

Alert Types

  • data-type="success" - Green: Success, confirmation messages
  • data-type="error" - Red: Error, danger, critical issues
  • data-type="warning" - Yellow/Amber: Warnings, cautions
  • data-type="info" - Blue: Information, tips, neutral notifications
