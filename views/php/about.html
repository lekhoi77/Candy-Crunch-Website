Our Story
The History & Journey of Candy Crunch
Our founder grew up around handcrafted street candies that weren’t perfect, but always carried a sense of innocence and laughter. Years later, in a market filled with industrial products, that feeling was gone. The absence of emotional delight inspired the creation of Candy Crunch — a brand designed to bring back the small, vibrant moments of happiness a single candy can create.

Candy Crunch Throughout the Years

2019
Birth of the Concept
Our founder began experimenting with crunchy candy at home, aiming to create a clean, joyful “crunch” sound that could brighten someone’s day.
2020
The First Successful Batch
After many failed attempts, two signature flavors emerged: Grape Pop and Strawberry Burst. Family and friends loved them immediately.
2021
“Candy Crunch” Gets Its Name
The name was chosen to reflect the core experience — the crunch. The first logo was hand-drawn, marking Candy Crunch’s official beginning.
2022
Launching to Customers
Candy Crunch started selling online. In the first month, 80% of orders came from WOM, proving emotional connection was the brand’s strength.
